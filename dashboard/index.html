    Data Provenance & Citations
    🇬🇭
    KNUST VS Lab — Primary
    1,998 respiratory disease cases from Kumasi VS Laboratory, 2018–2021. Monthly breakdown used for seasonal risk weights. Newcastle = 64.51% of cases.
    Mensah et al. (2023). PAMJ-One Health 12:11. DOI: 10.11604/pamj-oh.2023.12.11.40752
    🇬🇭
    Ashanti Farm Survey
    76 poultry farms in Ashanti Region. Source for biosecurity risk factors: 71.1% no footbath, 55.3% on-farm waste disposal. Used for biosecurity scoring rules.
    Ayim-Akonor et al. (2020). BMC Veterinary Research.
    🇬🇭
    Own-Collected Ghanaian Audio
    Audio recordings from Kofi's father's farm, Ashanti Region. Fine-tunes classifier to Ghanaian poultry house acoustics. GPS-tagged, date-stamped.
    Collected May 2026 · Ashanti Region · Confirmed healthy flock
    🇳🇬
    Bowen University Audio Dataset
    260 poultry audio clips (139 healthy, 121 sick). Primary CNN training. Nigerian data — Ghanaian fine-tuning applied on top via transfer learning.
    Bowen University Nigeria · Mendeley Data
    🇹🇿
    Tanzanian Droppings Dataset
    PCR-verified droppings photos, 34 farms, Arusha and Kilimanjaro. 3 classes used (Salmonella excluded). NCD class augmented ×4 to ~1,500 images.
    Zenodo record 5801834 · Nelson Mandela African Institution of Science and Technology
    🇬🇭
    Ghana VSD Schedule
    Official Ghana Veterinary Services Directorate vaccination schedule. Implemented verbatim for compliance scoring and automated WhatsApp reminder timing.
    Ghana VSD + supplementary protocol, Kofi's father's farm, Ashanti
