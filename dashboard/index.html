    Data Provenance & Citations
    🇬🇭
    KNUST VS Lab — Primary
    1,998 respiratory disease cases diagnosed at Kumasi Veterinary Services Laboratory, 2018–2021. Monthly breakdown used for seasonal risk weights. Newcastle = 64.51% of cases.
    Mensah et al. (2023). PAMJ-One Health 12:11. DOI: 10.11604/pamj-oh.2023.12.11.40752
    🇬🇭
    Ashanti Farm Survey
    76 poultry farms in Ashanti Region. Source of biosecurity risk factors: 71.1% no footbath, 55.3% on-farm waste disposal. Used for biosecurity scoring rules.
    Ayim-Akonor et al. (2020). Zoonotic disease risks, Ashanti Region. BMC Veterinary Research.
    🇬🇭
    Own-Collected Ghanaian Audio
    Audio recordings from Kofi's father's farm, Ashanti Region. Used to fine-tune audio classifier for Ghanaian poultry house acoustics. GPS-tagged, date-stamped.
    Collected May 2026 · Ashanti Region · Confirmed healthy flock

    Bowen University Audio Dataset
    260 poultry audio clips (139 healthy, 121 sick). Used for primary CNN classifier training. Nigerian data — Ghanaian fine-tuning applied on top.
    Bowen University Nigeria · Mendeley Data
    🇹🇿
    Tanzanian Droppings Dataset
    PCR-verified droppings photos from 34 farms, Arusha and Kilimanjaro regions. 3 classes used (Salmonella excluded). NCD class augmented ×4.
    Zenodo record 5801834 · Nelson Mandela AfricanInstitution of Science and Technology
    🇬🇭
    Ghana VSD Vaccination Schedule
    Official Ghana Veterinary Services Directorate vaccination schedule. Implemented verbatim in the vaccination tracker. Used for compliance scoring and reminder timing.
    Ghana VSD + supplementary protocol from Kofi's father's farm, Ashanti
